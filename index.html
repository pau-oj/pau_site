Trajectory

I come from a landscape defined by extreme contrast. Roots in the silent countryside, life in the friction of the city.


This duality wired my brain. I became a compulsive observer of the invisible codes of society. I realized that observing was no longer enough; I needed to build tools to reshape it.

> "I define myself as a maker who thinks with her hands, bridging the gap between high-fidelity technology and the low-fidelity reality of the Global South." [cite: 107]
