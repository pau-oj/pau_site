The Silent Archive.

01. INQUIRY

ORIGIN: PÁRAMO [3,200 MASL]

My research begins in the silence of the high Andes. Here, I investigate how code can preserve the "small data" of biological decay. I use computer vision to analyze the fading chromatics of endangered flora, creating a digital archive that breathes.

The Physics of Inequality.

02. CONTEXT

CONTEXT: BOGOTÁ [2,600 MASL]

Descending into the city, the silence breaks. This section documents "Ladrilludo", an acoustic probe disguised as a brick. By dragging sensors through the streets, I record the invisible weight of social stratification.

Recursive Mutation.

03. METHOD

METHOD: MAGDALENA [100 MASL]

In the heat of the river valley, technology must adapt or die. This is "Recursividad": hacking scarcity to produce abundance. Here, I display interfaces that sweat, mutate, and survive.
