> HYPOTHESIS: Can a brick record the sound of inequality?


Designed a "sensing brick" with embedded microphones to record urban soundscapes. This project gives physical weight to the concept of social mobility by dragging a brick through the city. [cite_start]Published as a collaborative paper on Object-Oriented Inquiry. [cite: 60, 133]


The device acts as an acoustic probe disguised as construction debris, recording the raw friction of the streets.
