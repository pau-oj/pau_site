[ APPLICANT STATEMENT ]

From the "I" of Art to the "We" of Social Engineering.

I come from a landscape defined by extreme contrast. My family roots are grounded in the Colombian countryside—a place of stoic silence—yet I grew up navigating the chaotic friction of the city. This duality wired my brain to be a compulsive observer.

"In the Global South, innovation is rarely about having the newest tool; it is about survival. It is the concept of Recursividad (resourcefulness): the ability to hack a solution when the supply chain breaks."

I define myself as a maker who thinks with her hands, constantly bridging the gap between high-fidelity technology and the low-fidelity reality of my context. I am applying to the MIT Media Lab (Critical Matter) to hybridize this resourcefulness with rigorous computation.
